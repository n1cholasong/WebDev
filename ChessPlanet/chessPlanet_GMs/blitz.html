Classical Classical Chess is a mode where each player can play the game slow and think through each move before they move their pieces. Game time may take more than 90 mins.
Rapid Rapid Chess is a quick mode where each player have a limited time to play. Game time may vary from 10 mins but no more than 60 mins.
Blitz Blitz Chess is a speed mode where each player have a very short time to play. Players do not have much time to think through each move. Game time may vary from 10 mins and below.
